## Ingredients:
	1 tsp Cayenne Pepper
	4 tsp Oregano
	4 tsp Sweet Paprika
	3 tsp Sugar or Rapadura
	4 tsp Rock Salt
	4 Tbsp Ground Cumin
	4 Tbsp Onion Flakes (dried)
	4 Tbs Garlic Powder/Granules

## Instructions:
	Mix in thermomix on speed 10 for 20-30 seconds
	Store in airtight container!
